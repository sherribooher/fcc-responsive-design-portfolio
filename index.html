About Me

Hi! My name is Sherri, and I am a software developer in Atlanta, Georgia. My love of technology was born one Christmas morning when I received my very first computer as a gift from Santa. In the mid-90s, I taught myself HTML and CSS and began to craft basic websites for friends and family. Over the years, I also dabbled with JavaScript and jQuery, learning just enough to be dangerous.

In 2017, I received my B.S. in Information Technology from Kennesaw State University, and shortly thereafter, I got my first job in the software industry, where I currently write code using VB.Net, C#, and SQL. I recently graduated from DigitalCrafts' Full Stack Software Engineering program, where I learned more about web technologies, languages, and frameworks than I ever thought possible! Having gained valuable skills over the years, I hope to now find and land my dream job with a company whose vision of the future aligns with my own. People can live better lives through the use of technology, and I hope to be a part of that in some way.
